About Me

Hello! I'm Emma, a front-end developer based in Toronto. As a graduate of HackerYou's immersive coding school I enjoy making beautiful, creative, and accessible software.

Before diving into web development I graduated from the University of Waterloo with a degree in Industrial Engineering and spent a year as a Product Manager. This role actively encouraged me to think like a developer as I was a designer, QA and laison between developers and the client.

With the technical skills I have gained at HackerYou and my experience delivering software products I'm excited to join a team as a developer!
